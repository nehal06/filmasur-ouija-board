Ouija Board

REG.U.S.PAT.OFF
The mystifying oracle

Ouija Board is also known as "Spirit Board" or "Talking Board" . It is a flat board marked with the letters of the alphabet, the number 0-9, the word "Yes", "No" and "Goodbye" along with various symbols and graphics. It uses a small heart shaped piece of wood called planchette.

Participants make a six corner star and also place candle on every corner of the star. They sits on the star place their fingers on the planchette and start asking question to the spirit. During this, the lights should be switched off and it is called to play the spirit board after 12 O'clock in night.

After playing the planchette should not be placed on the board. Their can be a little risk. Participants play the spirit board on their own risk. If something wents wrong during the play the ouija board should immediatly destroyed.
